# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all contributors, regardless of experience level, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or nationality.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Being respectful and inclusive
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other communication channels.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **contact@orhanyildirim.us**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers who do not follow the Code of Conduct may be removed from the project team.
